RISE: Philadelphia

After the murder of George Floyd, protests and riots erupted across the country, including Philadelphia. I attended a BLM protest from the Philadelphia Museum of Art, to Broad and Vine Streets, to City Hall. A friend and I photographed the protests and designed a photo book.
We have since sold 55 copies and have donated over $470 to local organizations serving Black communities.
